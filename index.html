FAQ
How can I get started with City Distro?

To get started, simply download our e-commerce app or connect with us via WhatsApp. Whether you’re a manufacturer looking for market access or a retailer seeking to stock up on high-quality goods, City Distro has you covered.

How can retailers use WhatsApp to interact with City Distro?

Retailers can order products, track deliveries, and communicate with City Distro via WhatsApp. . This feature provides a simple, user-friendly option for managing transactions on a platform they’re already familiar with.

What financial support does City Distro provide?

We offer working capital loans to retailers, enabling them to maintain operations and grow their businesses. Additionally, our inventory financing solution for manufacturers ensures they can stock up without upfront payments.

What makes City Distro’s products unique?

City Distro is committed to promoting wholly Nigerian-made goods. Our inventory showcases high-quality, locally-produced FMCG products, helping to support the local economy and reduce dependency on foreign imports.

How does the e-commerce app benefit retailers?

City Distro’s e-commerce app is an all-in-one platform where retailers can:
- Browse and order products from a wide inventory of African-made goods.
- Track deliveries in real-time.
- Manage store operations using the integrated management module.
- Access loans and manage finances through the electronic wallet.

What is City Distro Intelligence?

City Distro Intelligence is a data-driven tool designed to provide actionable insights for manufacturers and retailers. By analyzing supply chain performance, market trends, and operational efficiency, it helps businesses optimize processes, improve sales, and make informed decisions.

‍

How does City Distro ensure efficient delivery?

We operate an express delivery service that guarantees goods are delivered within 48 hours to retailers. Our hub-and-spoke model optimizes order tracking, reduces delivery times, and ensures last-mile delivery even to remote locations.

‍

How does City Distro’s inventory financing work?

City Distro’s "Buy Now, Pay Later" option allows manufacturers and retailers to access inventory without upfront payment. Businesses can stock up on products immediately and defer payments for a few says or weeks, until their cash flows improve, easing financial burdens and ensuring business continuity.

What exactly is City Distro?

City Distro is a Pan African distribution company dedicated to making African-made goods visible, accessible, affordable and dominant on the shelves of modern trade retail outlets across the continent. We aim to transform the FMCG landscape in Africa by supporting local manufacturers, retailers, and distributors through a decentralized, community-based retail network.
